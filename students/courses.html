Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program is a four-year degree program
which focuses on the study of computer utilization and computer software to plan, install, customize,
operate, manage, administer and maintain information technology infrastructure.


JAVA PROGRAMMING
WEB DEVELOPMENT
PYTHON PROGRAMMING
MOBILE BASED APPLICATION
DATA ANALYST

Bachelor of Science in Secondary Education

The BSEd is an undergraduate teacher education program designed to equip learners with
adequate and relevant competencies to teach in their chosen area of specialization/major in
the secondary level.


MAJOR IN ENGLISH
MAJOR IN SCIENCE
MAJOR IN MATHEMATICS
MAJOR IN FILIPINO
MAJOR IN ETHICS

Bachelor of Science in Elementary Education

Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd) is a four-year program that provides academic
and appropriate training for future elementary school teachers of Grades 1 – 6 through the general
education courses, professional education, and specialization courses.
